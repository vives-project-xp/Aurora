This is our way of creating the interactive hallway project

Our inspiration for this project, were the northern lights. We have created a interactive hallway using leds and sensors. These will follow the people surrounding them, and be able to display random effects. Via this site you will be able to control the LED bar. On this site you will also find our movieclip, some pictures of the progress, all the teammembers and the nescesarry components.
